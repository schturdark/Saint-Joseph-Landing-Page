This free app will help you to meditate on St. Joseph life, virtues, joys and sorrows. The content are based on the book “The Devout Year” by Rev. RF Clarke, S.J. and another book "Devotions to Saint Joseph" by Rev. Fr. Joseph Patrignani, S.J. This app helps you to meditate for a month and automatically select the meditation for today. There is a daily reminder feature to help you to meditate daily.
